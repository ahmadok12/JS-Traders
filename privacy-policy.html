2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information — your name, phone number, and email address when you fill in our contact form or reach out via WhatsApp.
  • Message content — the content of messages you send us through WhatsApp or our website form, including inquiries, orders, and feedback.
  • Business information — farm name, location, or product requirements you voluntarily share with us.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as pages visited and time spent on our website (no personally identifiable data is linked to this).

We do not collect payment card details, passwords, or sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide product information
  • To process and fulfil orders and related transactions
  • To send you transactional or service-related WhatsApp messages (e.g., order confirmations, delivery updates)
  • To improve our products, services, and customer experience
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

4. WhatsApp Messaging

JS Traders uses the WhatsApp Business API (provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.) to communicate with customers. By messaging us on WhatsApp, you acknowledge and agree to the following:
  • Your WhatsApp phone number and messages are processed by Meta in accordance with WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • We will only send you WhatsApp messages that are directly related to your inquiry, order, or a service you have requested.
  • We will never send you unsolicited promotional messages via WhatsApp without your prior opt-in consent.
  • You may opt out of WhatsApp communications at any time by replying STOP or by contacting us directly at jstraders.okr@gmail.com.
  • Message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

Our use of the WhatsApp Business API complies with Meta's WhatsApp Business Policy and applicable messaging guidelines.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party platforms (such as Meta/WhatsApp for messaging, and web hosting providers) that help us operate our business, under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the successor entity under the same privacy protections.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months unless an ongoing business relationship requires longer retention.
  • WhatsApp message history is retained as long as the conversation thread is active or for up to 12 months after the last interaction.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of WhatsApp communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jstraders.okr@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These measures include:

  • Using secure, encrypted communication channels where available
  • Limiting access to personal data to authorized personnel only
  • Using reputable third-party platforms (Meta, WhatsApp Business API) with their own security standards

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business customers and individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will indicate the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
